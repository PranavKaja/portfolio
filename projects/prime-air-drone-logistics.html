PRIME AIR DRONE LOGISTICS

A hub-and-spoke last-mile delivery model for suburban drone delivery.

Hub-and-SpokeGeospatial12-Month RoadmapConstraint Model

BRIEFING

A last-mile feasibility study for suburban drone delivery in Massachusetts. Demographic and geospatial analysis identified optimal hub placement under real constraints, payload limits, flight range, and urban no-fly restrictions, rather than assuming a blank map. The result is a hub-and-spoke network packaged as a 12-month phased rollout designed to cut last-mile lead times where the demand and the regulations actually allow it.

ROLE / METHOD / OUTCOME

Role
Network designer.
Method
Geospatial and demographic constraint analysis.
Outcome
Suburban hub-and-spoke model with a 12-month rollout plan.
