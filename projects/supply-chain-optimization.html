SUPPLY CHAIN OPTIMIZATION

34% cumulative margin across a four-year horizon, with zero stockouts.

34% Margin98% Service LevelZero StockoutsHybrid Sourcing

BRIEFING

Managed a simulated global supply chain through four years of high demand volatility. The strategy: a hybrid sourcing mix paired with statistical inventory buffering sized to the demand variance rather than gut feel. That combination held a 98% service level with zero stockouts while still maximizing gross margin, proof that resilience and profitability aren't a forced trade-off when the buffers are sized with data.

ROLE / METHOD / OUTCOME

Role
Strategy lead.
Method
Hybrid sourcing with statistical inventory buffering.

34% margin at a 98% service level, zero stockouts.
