BRIEFING

An end-to-end capstone on 70,000+ patient records. Ensemble classifiers reached 92% accuracy, with SHAP decomposition exposing the top risk drivers so a clinician could trust the call rather than accept a black-box score. The model was wrapped in a Flask web app for real-time risk scoring — moving it from notebook to something a user can actually interact with.

ROLE / METHOD / OUTCOME

Role
Capstone lead.
Method
Ensemble classification with SHAP decomposition, served via Flask.
Outcome
92% accuracy with per-patient explainability.
