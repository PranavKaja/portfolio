HOSPITAL READMISSION

73% critical-case detection through deliberate threshold tuning.

25K Records73% DetectionSHAP DriversThreshold Tuning

BRIEFING

Readmission risk scoring on 25,000 anonymized records. Rather than accept the default 0.5 cutoff, probability thresholds were tuned to lift critical-case detection to 73% while consciously balancing the precision–recall trade-off — in a clinical setting, a missed high-risk patient costs far more than a false alarm. SHAP surfaced inpatient-visit count and age as the dominant predictors, pointing discharge planning at the right patients.

ROLE / METHOD / OUTCOME

Role
Solo analysis.
Method
Logistic regression with tuned probability thresholds.
Outcome
73% of critical cases caught before discharge.
