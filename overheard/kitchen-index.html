HOW THIS LIST WAS MADE

The pipeline pulls comments from brands' own Instagram posts, classifies each one with Gemini (question, complaint, purchase intent, or noise), and ranks the flagged ones by a 0-100 response priority. Then the honest part: scrapers can't see reply threads, so every candidate for this page was checked by hand in the live thread. Of the 33 top-priority complaints checked, 17 had picked up a brand reply by check time, and nearly every one of those replies was a version of "please DM us." Three comments had been deleted or hidden. One was unclear. The rest, plus verified items from a separate random calibration sample, are the 20 below: no public reply of any kind as of the check date. Replies that arrive later are counted in the next report.
